Seid Hassen Yesuf (M.Sc.)

I am a lecturer at the Department of Computer Science,University of Gondar ,Ethiopia. I received MSc. from Addis Ababa University on July 2015 and BSc. at University of Gondar on July 2010.My research interests and activities over the past 5 years have been in Natural language processing(NLP) and Image Processing, especially word sense disambiguation,WordNet,social media analytics and machine learning applications to NLP and BioInformatics.

I was joined Wollo University as a Graduate Assistant in 2010. I taught computer science courses to Informatics college students till I transferred to University of Gondar in 2017.I also served as coordinator of research,community service and postgraduate program of Informatic college of Wollo University.

Currently, I am serving as a Head of Department of Computer Science to which I am accountable for directing the teaching, research and community service activities of the department and promote the enhancement of the quality and relevance of the teaching,research and community service in the department as well as create conductive atmosphere for the expansion of academic activities in the department.
